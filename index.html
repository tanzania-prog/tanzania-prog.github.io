Research

Where I spend my time

Safe Playbook Evolution for SEM Fracture Classification
Research Intern  ·  Nov 2025 – Present  ·  Qatar Environment & Energy Research Institute (QEERI)

Developed Safe Playbook Evolution (SPE), a guardrail framework for test-time adaptation of vision-language models on SEM fracture surface classification. Discovered and characterised rule dominance collapse, a novel failure mode in agentic context engineering. Achieved 89.24% accuracy on a 1,961-image held-out evaluation without model fine-tuning.

Vision-Language Models Agentic AI Test-time Adaptation Scientific ML
Gated Physics-Informed Neural Network for PV Forecasting
Research Intern  ·  May – Jul 2025  ·  Texas A&M University, College Station

Designed a Gated Physics-Informed Neural Network (CPINN) for photovoltaic power forecasting, incorporating a learnable gate that dynamically controls physics model contribution on a per-sample basis. Evaluated on real Hong Kong PV station data against data-only and static PINN baselines, revealing interpretable seasonal patterns in physics reliability.
